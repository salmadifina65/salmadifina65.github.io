My Hobbies

My Drawing

This is my digital and traditional drawings, I'm drawing in my free time and only when I feel like it. I only draw as a self-indulgement so I draw characters/in a style that I like only. The software I use to draw is IbisPaint X that I got from Play Store and I use my own phone to draw and by hand too. Sometimes the result isn't quiet as good, but I enjoy the experience. My digital and tradition art has different style so I'm not sure if I'm already consistent or not. And, I'm not good at painting or colouring so I only sketch when I do traditional drawing.

My Drawing

I do lettering as my go-to free time activity since I also love note taking and other stuff as well. I usually wrote lyrics from my favourite songs or a quote I found super cool. Material I used for lettering is brush pen and marker. I don't really understand colour or many fonts so it's pretty constant and look the same. I'm not really doing any lettering anymore since I don't really know where should I put the lettering, so yeah, I usually ended up throw them out after.
